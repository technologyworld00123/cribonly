About

I am a talented footballer and computer programmer, who loves to keep myself busy with my two passions. One day, I received exciting news that my sister was expecting her first child. Overjoyed at the prospect of becoming an uncle, I decided to help my sister and her husband with the preparations for the new arrival.
I wanted to buy a baby crib that would be perfect for my new nephew, but I soon found out that finding the right crib was not as easy as it seemed. I spent hours searching the internet and visiting stores, but nothing seemed to fit my sister's criteria. Frustrated and determined to find the perfect crib, I decided to use my programming skills to make the search easier.
I spent several days coding a program that would identify the ideal crib based on my sister's requirements, including size, color, and price. When the program was complete, I was thrilled to see the results. The program had found several cribs that fit my sister's criteria, and I quickly picked the one I liked best.
However, my excitement was short-lived when I realized that the crib was out of stock. Undeterred, I spent the next few days scouring the internet and calling different stores to find the same crib. Eventually, I found a store that had just restocked, and I rushed to make the purchase.
As I waited for the crib to arrive, I felt a sense of accomplishment in using my programming skills to solve a real-world problem. When the crib finally arrived, I was pleased to see that it was everything I had hoped for. My sister was overjoyed with the gift, and I felt proud to have played a part in welcoming my new nephew to the world.
From that day on, I continued to use my programming skills to make life easier for my family and friends. I realized that the combination of my passions for football and computer programming gave me a unique set of skills that I could use to help others.


Abdul Wahab - Developer and Editor

Abdul Wahab is the Founder of cribonly.com from Pakistan. He loves to do coding. He is a Computer Programmer from Lahore Garrison University.
